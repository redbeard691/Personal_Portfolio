Past

Growing up there was always an urge to build, whether it was with my Legos or building a wooden castle. I have about a decade's worth of work experience repairing, fabricating, and building various projects for customers. This has ranged from repairing personal projects for friends and CNC-machining prototype parts for a college rocket team to creating parts for the Department of Defense and other aerospace companies. I was even sent to Alaska to fabricate and build for companies in the fishing industry.

Due to medical reasons though, I was no longer able to physically keep up with the demands of these jobs and needed to make a career shift. Upon reflection, I realized I wanted to continue to build and repair things, but I needed a change in medium career wise. I wanted to try my hand with computers and software development.
